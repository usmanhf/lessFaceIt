About Us

The Challenge

Increase doctor-patient interaction by reducing the amount of time the doctor spends on the computer. (presented by BlueChasm)

Our Solution

We are creating a website that incorporates state of the art technology to streamline the extensive sign-in process. A camera will be used in conjunction with Microsoft’s Face API to recognize the doctors and patients. The respective information will then be pulled up, allowing for maximum interaction between the doctor and patient. Our name, Less Face It, is a play on the words, Let’s Face It, which refers to both facing the camera and the computer. We are saying to face the camera, but at the same time, we want to minimize the contact with the computer, therefore “less” face it.

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate sophomores from UT Austin and Texas A&M who want to make a difference. Helping to increase human-to-human interaction is what drives us to create new and unique ways of using technology.
